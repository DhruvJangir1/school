About

Christian Liberty Academy (CLA) in Suriname, which serves Pre-Kindergarten (K4) through Grade 12, follows a Christ-centered, biblical curriculum and uses an American high school curriculum. They are accredited by the National Association of Private Schools (NAPS). While the specific grading system isn't explicitly detailed in the search results, it is known that CLA uses an American high school curriculum. It is also the largest English-language school in Suriname and has become an established institution.
